About Pokemon Size Comparison

Compare the real-world sizes of any Pokemon side by side. All heights are sourced from official Pokemon Home data.

How to use: Type a Pokemon name to add it to the canvas. Use the Scale button to change the reference figure (human, child, dog, basketball, car, or door). Toggle Grid Lines for measurement reference. Click Zoom to switch between fit-to-screen and actual-size modes.

Measuring: On desktop, click and drag on any Pokemon image to draw a measurement line. Click the line again to remove it. Measurements are disabled in animated mode.

Units: Click any height label to toggle between metric and imperial.
