Semantic Search Pokemon Ontology

160,000 words of Pokedex descriptions, 1025 Pokemon, gotta tag em all

Coming Soon

Pop quiz, which Pokemon live near glaciers and contain fur? Who knows? For the past 30 years Pokemon flavor text has not been standardized, sometimes even recycling text. It's a major pain to scan through the 30-ish Pokedex entries of every Pokemon trying to find new information. Not to mention trying to compare separate Pokemon. Here are screenshots of the dozens programs AI coded for me to help make this project a reality. What started with a script to pull in all text entries then turned into another code to deduplicate entries. Which then led to another single-use program AI made to count word frequency of each Pokemon species, another tool to categorize, another to merge and keep track of terms because I don't have access to PoolParty - not that I'd use the advanced features anyway... I even signed up for a free Google API key.
